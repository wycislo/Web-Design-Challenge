Rice University Data Analytics Bootcamp

The data analytics bootcamp will expose students to a broad range of technologies that are used by data analists. You will work your rear end off learning Excel, Python, JavaScript, SQL, Git and API.

Additional skills are those you will acquire but will not be able to easily measure. Collaborative learning, improving how fast you learn, learning how to teach your something you don't yet understand, and how to present your discoveries.
