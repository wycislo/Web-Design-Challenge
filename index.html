This analysis will show how the weather changes as you get closer to the equator.

The data was assembled by accessing the OpenWeatherMap API. After the data was retrieved, I used matplotlib and pandas to show the various aspects of the data. Humidity, temperature, cloudiness, and windspeed were compared with the latitude of the data.
